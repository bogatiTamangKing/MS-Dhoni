Life of MS Dhoni

Early Life

Mahendra Singh Dhoni was born on 7 July 1981 in Ranchi, Jharkhand. He loved football and badminton but eventually found his way into cricket. His talent as a wicketkeeper and batsman was spotted early in local matches.

Career

Dhoni made his international debut for India in December 2004 against Bangladesh. He quickly rose to prominence with aggressive batting and cool-headed leadership. He captained India to the ICC T20 World Cup in 2007 and the ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011.

Achievements

  • #Won ICC T20 World Cup (2007)
  • #Won ICC Cricket World Cup (2011)
  • #Won ICC Champions Trophy (2013)
  • #Led Chennai Super Kings to multiple IPL titles
  • #Honored with Padma Bhushan, Padma Shri, and Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna awards
